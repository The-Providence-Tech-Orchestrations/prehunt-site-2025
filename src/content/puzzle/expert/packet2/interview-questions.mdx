<ul>

    How many potato chips have been consumed on the campus of MIT since its inception in 1861?
  </li>
  <li>How many drops of water are there in all of Europa's oceans?</li>
  <li>
    If, once the coin for the 2025 MIT Mystery Hunt was found, you hired someone to read the words
    in the Oxford English Dictionary (not including the definitions) as a full-time job, how many
    minutes from starting their job would it take them to read the answer to this puzzle? (Include
    time off.)
  </li>
  <li>How many grains of rice have ever been consumed in Asia?</li>
  <li>What is the combined age, in years, of all the authors of this question?</li>
  <li>
    How many atoms were in the ink molecules used to print this page? (Include the water solvent.)
  </li>
  <li>
    How likely is it that a monkey produces the exact answer to this puzzle using a typewriter on
    their first try (assuming said monkey is sure to avoid numbers and punctuation?) (Write the
    reciprocal of the probability.)
  </li>
  <li>
    How many microseconds would it take an able-bodied member of your team to sharpen a pack of
    pencils down to nubs with a manual pencil sharpener (assuming they were really trying and using
    their dominant hand?)
  </li>
  <li>How many bank robberies will there be in the US in 2024 (including Finster's?)</li>
</ul>
